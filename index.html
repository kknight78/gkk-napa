Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our stores and online bill pay.

How do I pay my G&KK NAPA commercial account bill online?

Visit our online bill pay page, enter your Account Number from your statement, the amount you want to pay, and your contact info. You can pay by bank transfer (ACH) with no fee, or by credit card with a small convenience fee.

What payment methods do you accept online?

We accept ACH bank transfers (no fee, up to $50,000) and credit/debit cards (2.9% + $0.30 convenience fee, up to $10,000). All payments are securely processed through Stripe.

Where are G&KK NAPA Auto Parts stores located?

We have four locations: Danville IL (303 N Gilbert St), Cayuga IN (307 N Logan St), Rockville IN (115 S Lincoln Rd), and Covington IN (205 4th St). All stores serve commercial accounts and walk-in customers.

What are your store hours?

Hours vary by location. Danville: Mon-Fri 7am-5:30pm, Sat 8am-5pm, Sun 10am-4pm. Indiana stores (Cayuga, Rockville, Covington): Mon-Fri 7-7:30am to 5-5:30pm, Saturday mornings, closed Sundays. See the Locations section for exact hours.
